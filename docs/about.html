About Keith Williams

The Journey

When I graduated from college during the .com bust in 2003, I experienced firsthand what it feels like when an old system collapses and a new one hasn't yet emerged.

That moment shaped everything that came after.

What I didn't realize then was that I was living through a pattern that repeats every 80 years in American history: 1787. 1865. 1945. And now, 2025.

For the past 22 years, I've been at the intersection of technology and education—first as a builder, then as an educator, and now as someone trying to help others navigate the biggest transition of our lifetime.

What I Do

Director, Enterprise AI Program - New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT)
I lead the development of production-grade AI systems and teach the next generation of technologists how to build responsibly and think strategically.

Production AI Systems Builder
Over the past three years, I've built working LLM systems—RAG architectures, knowledge graphs, autonomous agents—not as theory, but as enterprise software deployed in real environments.

Educational Innovator
I pioneered the "Code as Textbook" approach, teaching thousands of students to learn by building production systems, not by memorizing frameworks.

The Mission

We're at a once-in-80-years civilizational transition: - Three world-historic technologies (AI, Clean Energy, Biotech) are hitting tipping point - We're navigating $37 trillion in debt, inflation, and a labor-capital divide - Our education system was built for the last era, not this one

The Challenge: Help leaders understand where we are in history so they can navigate what's coming.

The Response: Rebuild education to create ethical, strategic, creative thinkers who can wield unprecedented technological power.

The Approach

I don't believe in telling people what to think. I believe in creating spaces where we can think together.

That's why I host town halls—no agenda, just dialogue. We explore hard questions, historical patterns, and practical strategies for navigating uncertainty.
