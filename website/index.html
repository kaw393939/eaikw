Here's The Problem

Companies have all the AI technology they could want. What they don't have? People who can actually implement it in the real world. There's this massive gap between "cool AI demo" and "production system that doesn't fall apart on Tuesday."

What We're Building

We're taking the solid foundations of Information Systems (databases, architecture, systems thinking) and adding modern AI on top. The result? Students who can design, build, and deploy smart systems that actually work—not just impress investors in a demo.

Graduates Will Be Prepared To:

  • ✓ Design, implement, and fine-tune machine learning and LLM models to solve business problems
  • ✓ Architect enterprise solutions that scale with AI capabilities
  • ✓ Apply responsible and ethical AI principles in system development and deployment
  • ✓ Lead teams through complex AI implementations
  • ✓ Communicate effectively with stakeholders at all levels

Industry Partnership Opportunities

Building industry connections for hiring pipeline, capstone projects, internships, and guest lectures. Training students for production AI implementation, not just demos.
